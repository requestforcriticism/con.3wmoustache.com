Events at 3 Wizards Con

3WC will be providing a variety of events throughout the weekend. These events will include but are not limited to the following:

Play-to-Win/Open Gaming

OVER 400+ GAME LIBRARY!

We've got a large variety of games that can be checked out over the weekend.
Checking out and playing a Play-to-Win game offers you the chance to win that game.

Scheduled Games

PLAN YOUR WEEKEND!

Host games for a discount on your badge.
Sign up for games and event to plan your weekend and save your spot.

Tournaments

PRIZES!

Participate in tournaments for a chance to win prizes. Every participate is guaranteed at least 1 raffle ticket.

Special Guests

MEET THE DESIGNERS!

We've got a handful of game designers that are here to show you what they have created. Our guests will be running demos and hosting panels throughout the weekend.

Vendors

ITEMS FOR SALE!

We've got a handful of vendors. Their items range from board games to local art.

Escape room

GOOD LUCK!

You and up to 3 friends will work together to solve the room in 40 minutes.

Panels

YOU SHALL BE ENTERTAINED!

We've got panels that are informative and entertaining.
Check them out.

Miscellaneous Games

NEVER ENDING GAMES!

All the other cool stuff that we couldn't fit in the other categories.

Video Games

PEW PEW!

The impressive video game section is provided by our sister con, Las Cruces Game Convention.
LCGC will be running video games thoughout the weekend.

Raffles

WIN STUFF!

We'll be giving out raffle tickets for many events. You can use these to win games and game themed items.
